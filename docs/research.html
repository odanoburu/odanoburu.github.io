Bruno Cuconato

[skip to publications]​

I am currently a PhD student/candidate/researcher at the computer science department at PUC-Rio, under the supervision of Edward Hermann Haeusler.

I mostly work on the Graph Logical Framework, a logical framework with a simple meta-language intended to facilitate the implementation and use (see the web interface) of deductive systems. This is in opposition to most logical frameworks (or proof assistants, as they like to be called) these days, whose focus lies more in the verification of the systems’ properties.

Previously I have worked with natural language processing, with a focus in developing datasets (under the guidance of Alexandre Rademaker). For my master’s thesis, I have developed a digital grammar for Portuguese under the Grammatical Framework formalism.

You can find my contact information in any of the papers below.
