Cloud Atlas⤴

by The Wachowskis, Tom Tykwer (2012)—watched

Synopsis

An exploration of how the actions of individual lives impact one another in the past, present and future, as one soul is shaped from a killer into a hero, and an act of kindness ripples across centuries to inspire a revolution.

Opinion

Probably my favorite film ever. It is so rich you can re-watch it several times—in fact it is recommended to do so since it’s too much to grab in only one sitting. Its stories thread a philosophy of life that I much enjoy.
